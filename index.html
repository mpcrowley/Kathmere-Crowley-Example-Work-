Advisor Command Center

A polished internal dashboard prototype built for Kathmere, designed to help leadership and advisors see where workflow friction is building. The command center tracks meeting readiness, overdue follow-ups, advisor workload, and operational risk using mock CRM-style data, then generates rule-based attention items and a weekly operations brief. The prototype demonstrates dashboard thinking, data visibility, and how AI could eventually summarize and prioritize workflow risks from HubSpot and Microsoft 365 data.

ScoreSync demonstrates the potential for a lightweight live-scoring platform that improves result visibility, reduces administrative friction, and creates a foundation for fixtures, data collection, and community engagement.

Mindful Golf

A local-first HTML MVP for a golf journaling app with an AI-style mental performance coach. Mindful Golf helps players log rounds, practices, tournament experiences, drill scores, swing notes, and reflection prompts, then generates rule-based coaching feedback to identify patterns in focus, commitment, patience, and performance. The prototype demonstrates how structured journaling and lightweight AI logic can turn everyday golf notes into actionable mental-game insights without relying on external APIs or complex backend systems.
