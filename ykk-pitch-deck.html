Market Position

~45% of the global premium fastener market, with sticky, long-tenured customers.

Regulatory Tailwind

EU Ecodesign 2024 + CS3D force documentation of durability and traceability — exactly what YKK already has.

02 — Proposition

A consulting service to mid-sized industrial manufacturers turns this know-how into revenue without cannibalising the core.

How we got here

Applied the Business Model Navigator (Gassmann et al., 2020). Three patterns fell away: Circular Economy, Customer Data, White Label. Make More of It is what remains.

Who

Mid-sized manufacturers in automotive supply, medical devices, precision electronics (€50–500m revenue) — too small for McKinsey/BCG, too technically demanding for generic consultants.

What

Paid diagnostics, process redesign, ramp-up support at day rates with outcome-linked fees tied to agreed KPIs.

Why defensible

AWS and Festo proved the pattern works. Talon, SBS, and Riri cannot replicate YKK's practitioner depth.
